Who we are

The LASEC Group is a leading structural steel engineering firm in Malawi. Well organized, managed and financed, the firm stays true to its founding principles, whilst being ever-ready to adapt to the constant changes and new challenges it faces in its external environment.

Services we provide

LASEC provides a comprehensive range of services within the structural steel industry, which can be summarized into the following categories:

Steel works

  • Traditional building of large steel products
  • Structural and general steel works
  • Rehabilitation, restoration and commercial maintenance
  • Manufacture of standard and customized window and door frames

Specialist services

  • Hire of Plant Equipment
  • Manufacture of fitted kitchens and bathrooms
  • Manufacture & Supply of School Furniture
  • Manufacture & Supply of Concrete Products

Interlocking

  • Manufacture & Supply of Interlocking Bricks

Tile adhesive

  • Manufacture & Supply of Tile Adhesive
  • LASEC Tile Adhesive is a Portland Cement based polymer modified quick set Ceramic Tile Adhesive specially designed for all variety of applications for fixing all tiles on both internal & external wall and floor surfaces.
  • This high performance Tile Adhesive also has an excellent waterproofing ability. When properly applied, it takes care of hollowness problem as well as giving excellent bond on cementitious surfaces like concrete and plaster
